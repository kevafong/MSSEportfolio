About Me

I'm Kevin Fong, a Master's recipient for the MSSE Program at the University of California, Berkeley. I have been working in at various biotech industries for the past 5 years as a Research Associate/Scientist. I developed an enthusiasm in using programming to build strong data analysis and processing tools to facilitate our role as researcher, and I decided to pursue the MSSE program to develop those skills through this program. I have a strong technical background in chemical and biomolecular studies, and I have been a quick learner to apply software engineering to my foundational background. I have a deep interest in C++ and molecular dynamics simualations, but am also deeply interested in analyses of biomoleulcar data.
